Gender Rules

Taking the Identify Gender quiz will teach you the 44 rules below to help you identify noun genders. These rules will help you identify the gender for nearly 3,000 of the 5,000 most commonly used German nouns. The other 2,000 nouns are either exceptions to a rule or are not covered by one of these rules so they must be learned individually.

Only rules that apply to at least 10 of the top 5,000 nouns and are correct at least 60% of the time are included. Most of the rules are not correct 100% of the time so the actual percentage correct for the top 5,000 nouns is shown in the table. The rules are sorted so that the most helpful come first. Helpfulness is a combination of how many nouns the rule applies to, how commonly used those nouns are, and how often the rule is correct.
